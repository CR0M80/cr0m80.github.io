## 📌 Overview

From **08-11-2025 to 07-12-2025**, I had the opportunity to lead the **Forensics sessions** during a 1-month bootcamp including 6 differents categories.

This bootcamp was organized by our club **GCDSTE (ENSA Marrakech)** in collaboration with **N7SEC (ENSET Mohammedia)**.

---

## 🎯 My Role

I was responsible for teaching **Digital Forensics**, including:

- 📚 Theoretical concepts  
- 🧪 Practical labs  
- 🔍 Real-world investigation techniques  

Each session focused on helping students understand how to analyze digital evidence.
